About Garissa Doctors Plaza

Committed to serving the community of Garissa

At Garissa Doctors Plaza, we believe that healthcare is a community right. Established with the mission to serve the people of Garissa, our hospital offers a range of outpatient services designed to meet the diverse needs of our patients.

Our compassionate staff is committed to providing personalized care, ensuring that every patient feels valued and understood. We are proud to be part of the Garissa community and strive to build lasting relationships with our patients and their families.
